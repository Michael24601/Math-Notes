    \section*{Exercise 3}
    $Q_8$ can be generated by $i$ and $j$, where: \\
    $\begin{rcases}
    1 = 1 \hspace{2em} \\
    -1 = i^2 \\
    i = i \\
    -i = i^3 \\
    j = j \\
    -j = j^3 \\
    k = ij \\
    -k = ji \\
    \end{rcases} Q_8 = \langle i, j
    \mid i^2 = j^2, i^4 = 1, i = jij \rangle$ \\
